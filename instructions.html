Installation Instructions

Follow these easy steps to get Wyvern Ex up and running.

01

Purchase Wyvern Ex

Visit our purchase page to buy Wyvern Ex.

Tip: Keep your purchase confirmation email safe. You'll need the license key for activation, and support may request it if you encounter any issues.
02

Download the Loader

Go to our download page and click 'Download Loader'. Once downloaded, right-click the .zip file and select 'Extract All'. When prompted, enter the password: 'wyvern'.

Note: Your browser or antivirus may flag the download as a potential threat. This is a false positive due to the way the application functions. If your browser blocks the file, go to the Downloads tab and click 'Keep' or 'Allow' to proceed. For proper antivirus exclusion setup, refer to the README file included in the download. Need help? Watch this guide.
03

Run the Loader

Launch the loader and enter the key from your confirmation email (e.g., WAS5AZ2LVU4F).

After that, start Gorilla Tag. Once the game has fully loaded, click the 'Inject' button.

Important: Gorilla Tag must be running before injecting, or it won't work.
04

Enjoy

Once the injection is complete, the mod menu will be ready to use automatically.
